Meet Aakarsh

Law graduate turned AI strategist. After 5 years in litigation and legal drafting—including clearing the Rajasthan Judicial Services Prelims and practicing at a High Court chamber in New Delhi—I pivoted to the intersection of artificial intelligence, human psychology, and governance frameworks.

That pivot wasn't a leap of faith. It was a logical extension. Law taught me to think in structures—precedent, burden of proof, adversarial testing. Psychology taught me why humans make irrational decisions even when the evidence is clear. AI gave me a medium where both disciplines converge.

Through Human Layer AI, I build systems that are psychologically informed, legally defensible, and genuinely human-centered.
